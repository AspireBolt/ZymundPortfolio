Project Overview

A borrowing and inventory system designed for AMT to efficiently manage tool and equipment loans, while maintaining detailed records of the students using them.

Key Features

Streamlined Borrowing

Simplifies lending and returns of tools and equipment for students and staff.

Detailed Usage Tracking

Maintains accurate records of items, borrowers, and return dates for accountability.
