Project Overview

CompEd is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between students and alumni by providing an easy way to connect through social media and job hunting features.

It creates a vibrant community where users can network and explore professional opportunities seamlessly.

In addition to fostering connections, CompEd integrates event management tools to help organize and promote campus activities efficiently.

It also includes a borrowing management system that tracks equipment and resource loans to ensure smooth and accountable usage.

The platform supports a secure payment system that simplifies transactions related to events and contributions.

Furthermore, CompEd offers an E-Learning page that enables students, faculty, and officers to access course materials, participate in quizzes, and earn certifications, supporting a collaborative and productive academic environment.
