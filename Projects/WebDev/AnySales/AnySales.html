Project Overview

A Point of Sales with built with security, management, inventory, cash register system with receipt printing, & offers many more in this site.

Key Features

Secure Transactions

Advanced security measures to protect sales data, payment information, and prevent unauthorized access.

Inventory Management

Track product quantities, manage stock levels, and receive low-stock alerts to ensure smooth operations.

Cash Register System

Fast and reliable checkout process with product scanning, discount application, and transaction logging.

Receipt Printing

Instantly print detailed receipts with custom branding, transaction IDs, and customer information.
